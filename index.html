About Us

The Learning Tree Project was founded to bridge the education gap in underserved areas of India through practical, community-led initiatives.

We partner with local schools, educators, and registered nonprofits to provide essential resources — from learning kits to tutoring support and school funding assistance.

Our vision is a future where every child, regardless of background, has the tools and support to learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Journey

Programs & Projects

Learning Kits (partner-supported)

We distribute basic learning kits (notebooks, stationery, and hygiene items) to students in collaboration with our partner nonprofits. These distributions are small, community-driven initiatives organized with local schools and volunteers.

School Funding Assistance

Providing targeted support for essential school needs — classroom supplies, repair of basic infrastructure, and small grants — carried out in partnership with registered nonprofit organizations on the ground.

After-School Tutoring

Local volunteers and student tutors run low-scale tutoring sessions focused on core subjects. These are pilot programs developed with guidance from partner NGOs and local educators.

Community Outreach

We organize local awareness drives and simple fundraising events (walks, bake sales, charity sports) to engage communities and raise modest funds — always coordinated with our nonprofit partners.

Pilot Digital Literacy Workshops

Small-scale workshops teaching basic computer and smartphone skills. These are early pilot efforts run with partner organizations and local schools rather than large technology rollouts.

School Supply Drives

Coordinated collection drives to source uniforms, books, and backpacks — organized with partner nonprofits to ensure proper distribution and local oversight.
